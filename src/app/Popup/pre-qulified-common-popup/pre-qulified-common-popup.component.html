Rejected

What it means: Your application didn’t meet one or more mandatory parameters or validation criteria.

What happens next: The application is closed and can’t be processed further under Criteria Based Lending.
close

In Progress

What it means: Your application is still under review.

What happens next: It will move through multiple evaluation stages until a final decision is made.

Journey stages (within “In Progress”)

GST Validation

What happens: GST details are fetched using the PAN provided.

What’s checked: Filing status, filing delays, and overall compliance.

Bureau Profile

What happens: Bureau profiles are reviewed for the company and associated directors (if applicable).

What’s checked: Credit history, repayment behaviour, delays/defaults, and overall creditworthiness.

Additional Information (Optional)

What happens: You can share extra documents (like GST documents, ITR information, bank statements) to support the applications for non-MCA registered entities.

Note: This step is optional for MCA Registered entities and only applies if you choose to provide more details.

Final Assessment

What happens: The rule engine runs and final eligibility for each Product is assessed.

Note on timelines: Processing may take longer if we’re waiting for responses from external systems (e.g., MCA Financials, Customs, Bureau, NCLT, etc)
close

Disabled

What it means: Application is incomplete and no activity was recorded for 60 days since.

What happens next: The application is automatically marked as Disabled.
